Description

Trace Invaders was a challenge my team and I worked on for the 2017 NASA SpaceApps Hackerthon. I worked alongside another developer on the Unity3D application to support our teams idea.

Our solution aimed to provide a social platform that used crowdsourcing methods to collect data and analytics about invasive species. This data could then be used with the Google Tango mixed relatity suite to create an interactive experience for students in schools
